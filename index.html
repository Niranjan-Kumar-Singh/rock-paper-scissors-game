Rock Paper Scissors

📘How to Play

Rock Paper Scissors is a simple hand game played between two people — in this case, you and the computer.

  • 🪨 Rock beats ✂️ Scissors
  • 📄 Paper beats 🪨 Rock
  • ✂️ Scissors beats 📄 Paper

Choose your move by clicking a button. The computer will randomly choose its move. Win more rounds than the computer to win the match!

You can choose “Best of 3”, “5”, or “7” from the dropdown. After the game ends, click “Start New Game” to play again.
