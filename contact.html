What Kind of Messages Do We Handle?

To help us serve you better, here is a quick guide on what types of inquiries we typically receive and how we handle them:

  • 🐛 Bug Reports: If you notice something broken — a game logic error, a visual glitch, or a broken link — please let us know with a brief description and the device/browser you are using. We typically patch bugs within 24–72 hours.
  • 💡 Feature Requests: Have an idea to make the game better? We love hearing them. Features that receive multiple requests get prioritized on our roadmap.
  • 📱 Performance Issues: If the game is slow or unresponsive on your device, sharing your device model and browser version helps us diagnose and fix performance bottlenecks faster.
  • 🤝 Collaboration & Business Inquiries: For partnerships, sponsored content, or development collaborations, please email us directly with "Business Inquiry" in the subject line for a faster response.
  • 📰 Press & Media: Bloggers, journalists, or content creators interested in covering or featuring this project are welcome to reach out for interviews or assets.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How quickly will you respond to my message?

We aim to respond to all messages within 24–48 hours during business days (Monday–Friday). Messages sent over the weekend are typically addressed by Monday. If you haven't heard back in 72 hours, please send a follow-up — it's possible your message ended up in our spam folder.

Q: Can I request a custom version of the game for my website or school?

Yes! We have received requests from educators and community organizations wanting a custom-branded or modified version of the game. Drop us a message with your requirements and we'll discuss what's possible.

Q: I found a serious security vulnerability — how do I report it responsibly?

Please email us directly at niranjansingh1419@gmail.com with "Security Report" in the subject line. Do not post security vulnerabilities publicly until we have had the chance to patch them. We appreciate responsible disclosure.

Q: Is the source code of this game available?

The game is hosted on Vercel and the code is visible in the browser's developer tools. If you are a developer interested in understanding how it works or building something similar, feel free to reach out — we're always happy to discuss the technical implementation.

Q: Can I contribute translations for different languages?

We would love to make the game accessible in multiple languages. If you're interested in contributing a translation, please get in touch via the form above or by email and we will share the relevant text strings with you.
